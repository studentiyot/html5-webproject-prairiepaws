Our Mission

Prairie Paws+Claws saves wildlife and inspires everyone to make conservation a priority in their lives.

Land Acknowledgment

Prairie Paws+Claws recognizes that these are the lands of the Tribal signatories of the Treaties of Kaskaskia and Big Muddy rivers. We acknowledge their stewardship of this place continues to this day and that it is our responsibility to join them to restore the relationship with the living world around us.
